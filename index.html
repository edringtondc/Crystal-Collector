Crystal Collection Game

Game Rules

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific calue to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the goal number.

You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game re-starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
